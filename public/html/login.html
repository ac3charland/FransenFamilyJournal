Welcome to the Fransen Family Journal!

First, a few tips & tricks on how to use the site:
Browse stories.

Click on any story on the home page to view it in its entirety.

To return to the home page, you can click the back button at the top of each entry or the "Fransen Family Journal" link at the top of the page anywhere on the site.

Register to write entries.

In order to submit entries to the journal, you need to register. Thankfully, registering is easy!

At the top of the page, click on the Login/Register link and enter your email and desired password in the "Register" form.

When you come back to the site, you can log back in using the "Log In" form on the same page.

Write an entry using our simple form.

To write your entry, click the "Write An Entry" link at the top of the page.

Then, fill out the author, title, and text of your entry, and attach an optional picture.

Are you helping someone submit an entry? You can enter their name as the author instead of your own.

If you have any questions or issues while using the site, feel free to email Alex and he'll be happy to help. Enjoy!
