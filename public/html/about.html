About

Welcome to the Fransen Family Journal!

For years, the descendants of Nicholas and Tina Fransen have kept in touch via a yearly email newsletter. Now, it’s time to carry that tradition into the 21st century with a dedicated website for the journal.

Adding your entry to the journal is easy: first, log in or register. Then, click on "Write An Entry" in the navbar. Then, create and submit your entry using the site's simple form.

This site was built by Alex Fransen Charland, son of Tom Charland and Charlotte Fransen and grandson of Herb and Mary Fransen. Features and performance improvements will be continually added to the site in the coming months—if you have any ideas for additions you’d like to see, feel free to email Alex. You should also email him if you encounter any issues while using the site so they can be promptly fixed!

Happy reading, and enjoy!

Welcome to the Fransen Family Journal!

First, a few tips & tricks on how to use the site:
Browse stories.

Click on any story on the home page to view it in its entirety.

To return to the home page, you can click the back button at the top of each entry or the "Fransen Family Journal" link at the top of the page anywhere on the site.

Register to write entries.

In order to submit entries to the journal, you need to register. Thankfully, registering is easy!

At the top of the page, click on the Login/Register link and enter your email and desired password in the "Register" form.

When you come back to the site, you can log back in using the "Log In" form on the same page.

Write an entry using our simple form.

To write your entry, click the "Write An Entry" link at the top of the page.

Then, fill out your name, the title of your entry, the text of your entry, and attach an optional picture.

You can add line/paragraph breaks in the text editor by hitting the ENTER key, and they will be displayed on the website when you submit.

If you have any questions or issues while using the site, feel free to email Alex and he'll be happy to help. Enjoy!
