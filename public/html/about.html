Welcome to the Fransen Family Journal!

For years, the descendants of Nicolas and Tina Fransen have kept in touch via a yearly email newsletter. Now, it’s time to carry that tradition into the 21st century with a dedicated website for the journal.

This site was built by Alex Fransen Charland, son of Tom Charland and Charlotte Fransen and grandson of Herb and Mary Fransen. Features will continually be added to the site in the coming months—if you have any ideas for any additions you’d like to see, feel free to email Alex. You should also email him if you encounter any issues while using the site so they can be promptly fixed!
